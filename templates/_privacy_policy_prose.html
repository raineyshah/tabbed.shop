What we collect

We collect your IP address, pages visited, filters clicked, and buy button clicks when you use the site. If you create an account we also store your email, account activity, and the products you save as favorites. You can choose to make your favorites publicly visible.

How we use it

To operate the site, improve the product, and attribute affiliate commissions to contributors.

What we may share with partners

We may share anonymized, aggregated trend reports with partners such as which product categories or certifications are most popular. These reports contain no personally identifiable information and cannot be used to identify you.

What we don’t sell

Your personal data such as your IP address, account information, and individual activity is never sold to third parties.

Who we share data with

Affiliate networks and merchants receive click data for commission tracking.

How long we keep it

Raw activity logs are deleted after 12 months. Aggregated reports contain no personal data and are retained indefinitely.

Your rights

You can request a copy of your data or ask us to delete it by contacting us at support@tabbed.shop.
